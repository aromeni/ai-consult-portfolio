# Build 8 — AI Adoption Delivery and Implementation Tracker
## Completion Review

---

### Final Purpose

Build 8 is the delivery layer of the AI adoption consulting portfolio. It takes the outputs of Builds 1, 4, 5, 6, and 7 — readiness findings, training plans, consulting reports, governance recommendations, and ROI evidence — and turns them into a tracked, owned, structured implementation plan.

The tool is designed for an AI adoption consultant managing post-audit delivery work with small and medium organisations. It provides everything needed to run a structured client review: action tracking, blocker identification, governance sign-off monitoring, training follow-up planning, client check-in summaries, implementation progress reports, and evidence exports.

### Consulting Value

Build 8 demonstrates that an AI adoption consultant can do more than produce recommendations. It shows the delivery side of the work: tracking what actually gets implemented, identifying what is blocking progress, monitoring governance and training obligations, and producing structured evidence for client reviews.

The tool is portfolio evidence that the consultant understands delivery risk, not just readiness analysis. It fills the gap between audit (Build 1), training (Build 4), reporting (Build 5), governance (Build 6), and impact measurement (Build 7) — and shows what happens after those outputs are produced.

---

### How Build 8 Connects to Earlier Builds

**Build 1 — AI Readiness and Workflow Audit**

Build 1 produces readiness findings and candidate workflow opportunities. Build 8 tracks whether those opportunities have been assigned an owner, converted to actions, and progressed to completion. Actions linked to Build 1 appear in the delivery progress, blocker, and governance sections of the Phase 7 report.

**Build 4 — AI Staff Training and Workshop Generator**

Build 4 produces training plans and confidence-building materials. Build 8 tracks whether that training has been followed up, whether staff confidence is being monitored, and whether further coaching or workflow demonstrations are needed. Training follow-up actions linked to Build 4 appear in the Phase 5 and Phase 7 outputs.

**Build 5 — AI Consulting Report Generator**

Build 5 produces consulting findings and client-facing recommendations. Build 8 converts those recommendations into owned implementation actions. The consulting report output becomes delivery evidence as each action is assigned, progressed, reviewed, and completed.

**Build 6 — AI Governance Policy Checker**

Build 6 identifies governance gaps and control recommendations. Build 8 tracks whether those gaps have been closed: whether sign-offs have been obtained, controls have been implemented, and approval owners have been engaged. Governance actions appear in the Phase 4 and Phase 7 outputs.

**Build 7 — AI Adoption ROI and Impact Tracker**

Build 7 measures adoption value and makes stop, continue, review, or scale decisions. Build 8 tracks whether those decisions are being acted on. Adoption decision actions appear in the delivery progress and client check-in sections of the Phase 7 report.

### Limitations

- **Synthetic data only.** Build 8 uses fictional organisations, actions, and check-ins. It does not connect to real client data, databases, APIs, or project management tools.
- **Deterministic logic only.** All classifications, scores, and recommendations are rule-based. No predictive modelling, machine learning, or LLMs are used.
- **No persistence.** Data resets each time the app is restarted. Action progress cannot be updated or saved between sessions.
- **No authentication.** Build 8 is a portfolio demonstration tool, not a multi-user or production system.
- **No real-time data.** All figures are based on the static synthetic records created in Phase 1.
- **No financial claims.** Build 8 does not include audited ROI figures or financial projections.

---

### Recommended Future Extensions

For a production-grade version of Build 8, the following extensions would add significant consulting value:

1. **Live data connection** — Connect to a real action register, project management tool (e.g. Asana, Notion, Trello), or spreadsheet data source.
2. **Progress updates in-app** — Enable a consultant to update action status, log check-ins, and record blockers directly within the tool.
3. **Role-based access** — Separate consultant and client views so clients can see their own progress without exposing the full implementation register.
4. **Timeline or Gantt view** — Add delivery scheduling to show when actions are due relative to each other.
5. **Branded PDF reporting** — Extend the export centre to produce client-branded PDF progress reports.
6. **Notification and reminder support** — Flag overdue actions or escalations to the consultant by email or Slack.
7. **Portfolio analytics** — Aggregate delivery progress across all organisations in a single portfolio dashboard.
