# BrightPath Skills Training AI Readiness Report

---

## Client Context

BrightPath Skills Training is a synthetic UK training provider with 24 staff across delivery, learner support, operations, compliance, and leadership functions. The organisation is exploring AI use in planning, administration, and support workflows.

---

## Challenge

BrightPath has early informal AI activity but limited governance maturity. The synthetic audit highlights unclear data boundaries, weak risk controls, staff training needs, and the need to avoid AI use with learner, safeguarding, HR, or confidential client information.

---

## Solution

Build 5 converts the synthetic audit findings into a complete consulting deliverable pack:

- AI readiness interpretation
- AI risk register
- Opportunity and pilot portfolio
- 30/60/90-day implementation roadmap
- Client-facing report sections
- Full Markdown client report
- PDF consulting report
- PowerPoint executive deck
- Completion review and portfolio notes

---

## Outputs Generated

- Readiness score summary and strategic interpretation
- Risk register with controls and owners
- Pilot recommendation sequence
- Implementation roadmap
- Training and capability needs
- Governance recommendations
- Immediate next steps
- Responsible-use boundaries
- Exportable report and executive deck

---

## Consulting Value

The case study shows how an AI consultant can move from audit findings to a practical client conversation. It helps the client see the readiness picture, the biggest risks, the safest pilot route, and the actions required before scaling AI adoption.

---

## Responsible-Use Controls

- Synthetic/demo data only
- No real learner data
- No safeguarding case information
- No confidential client records
- No staff HR data
- No personal data
- No regulated information
- No external LLM API calls
- Human review required before real-world use

---

## Limitations

- Synthetic scenario only
- Local prototype only
- No authentication
- No persistent storage
- No cloud deployment
- No real-data ingestion
- Not legal, safeguarding, HR, compliance, financial, medical, or professional advice

---

## Next-Step Recommendation

Use the generated report pack as a portfolio demonstration and learning artefact. Before any real client use, define a governance model, assign responsible owners, complete privacy/security review, and redesign the app for controlled real-data handling.
