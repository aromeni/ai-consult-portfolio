# Build 9 — Portfolio Summary

## Problem It Solves

Small organisations adopting AI typically receive fragmented advice: a readiness assessment from one tool, a training session from another, a governance review from a third. There is no single consulting view that connects these touchpoints into one coherent story, tracks progress across them, or produces shareable evidence that the adoption journey is being managed responsibly.

Build 9 solves this by providing the capstone layer that wraps all seven consulting service areas into one structured portfolio view, consulting report, and downloadable evidence pack.

---

## Target Users

- **Consultants and freelancers** offering AI adoption services to small organisations, who need a way to demonstrate their methodology and track client progress.
- **Portfolio reviewers and technical evaluators** assessing whether the consultant can design, build, and position a coherent consulting product.
- **Prospective clients** who want to understand what the consulting journey will look like before committing to a paid engagement.

## Consulting Use Cases

1. **Portfolio demonstration** — Run the Streamlit app in front of a prospective client or recruiter to walk them through the full AI adoption consulting methodology in 10 minutes.
2. **Business development** — Download the Markdown or PDF capstone report and send it as a leave-behind after a discovery conversation.
3. **Client onboarding** — Use the client journey overview and recommendation pathway as the starting point for a real consulting engagement, replacing synthetic data with actual client evidence.
4. **Evidence submission** — Download the JSON evidence pack and submit it as structured consulting evidence for a portfolio review or tender response.
5. **Commercial positioning** — Use the commercial positioning notes and Export Centre to articulate the consulting service as a structured, repeatable, evidence-backed methodology.

---

## Why Synthetic Data Was Used

Synthetic data was used throughout Build 9 for three reasons:

1. **No real client data is available** at this stage of the portfolio. Using synthetic data avoids the risk of accidentally including confidential, personal, or regulated information.
2. **Determinism.** Synthetic data allows every logic function to be tested with known inputs and expected outputs, making the test suite reliable and reproducible.
3. **Demonstration clarity.** Synthetic clients (BrightPath, Northside, Greenacre) are designed to produce different journey health outcomes, so the dashboard and report show the full range of consulting situations the system can handle.

---

## What It Demonstrates Commercially

Build 9 demonstrates:

- That the consulting practice thinks structurally about AI adoption, not just technically.
- That the practice can produce client-facing documents, dashboards, and evidence packs from a consistent analytical foundation.
- That the practice understands governance and responsible use — not just capability.
- That the methodology is repeatable: the same logic applies to any client, with different data producing different consulting outcomes.
- That the practice can package its work as a product — not just deliver it as a bespoke engagement.
