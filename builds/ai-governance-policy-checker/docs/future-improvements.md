## What Will Not Be Added (Without Full Governance Controls)

- Real client policy processing without data protection and governance controls
- Automated compliance certification or sign-off
- AI advice presented as legal, safeguarding, HR, or professional compliance advice
- Production deployment without appropriate security, access control, and governance sign-off
- External LLM or AI API integration without strict evidence controls and human review gates
