## Safety Boundaries

- Synthetic/demo policy data only — no real client data
- No real client policies, learner data, safeguarding case data, HR data, personal data, or regulated information
- No external LLM, AI, or third-party API calls
- No document upload
- No authentication
- No persistent storage
- No production deployment
- Not legal, safeguarding, HR, compliance, data-protection, financial, or professional governance advice
- Not a compliance certification system
- Human review required before any real-world use

See `docs/safety-boundaries.md` for full details.

---

## Known Limitations

- Synthetic data only — keyword coverage does not assess legal adequacy
- Coverage scores are indicative, not compliance ratings
- Gap analysis does not represent legal or regulatory risk findings
- Maturity scores are relative guidance, not certifications
- Wording directions require qualified review before use
- Prototype only — not for production or regulated use
