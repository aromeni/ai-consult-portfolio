# Safety Boundaries — Document Intelligence / RAG Demo

**Build 2 · BrightPath ChatGPT Mastery Project**

This document defines what this prototype may and may not be used with.
These boundaries must be maintained in all phases of this build.

---

## What this prototype may be used with

- Synthetic, fictional, or entirely made-up documents created for demonstration purposes
- Anonymised documents where all identifying information has been fully removed
- Publicly available documents that contain no personal or confidential data
- Documents you have created yourself using only general or fictional content

## What this prototype must never be used with

### Learner data
Do not enter, upload, or paste any learner data into this tool. Learner data includes:
- Names, dates of birth, contact details, or any identifying information about learners
- Attendance records, assessment grades, or progress information
- Health, disability, or special educational needs information
- Any data that could identify a learner directly or indirectly

### Safeguarding case information
Do not enter any safeguarding information. This includes:
- Disclosures, concerns, or referrals about abuse, neglect, or exploitation
- Information about vulnerable learners or children at risk
- Child protection records or case notes of any kind
- Information about contact with police, social services, or health services

### Confidential client records
Do not enter confidential client information. This includes:
- Contractual terms, pricing, or commercially sensitive information
- Client-specific strategy, planning, or proprietary information
- Client complaints, incidents, or performance records

### Staff HR data
Do not enter any staff HR or employment information. This includes:
- Names, contact details, or personal data of staff members
- Performance reviews, appraisals, or development records
- Disciplinary records, grievances, or complaints
- Salary, leave, or contractual terms

### Personal data
Do not enter any personal data about any identifiable individual — staff, learner, client,
carer, or member of the public. Personal data means any information that could identify
a living person directly or indirectly.

### Regulated information
Do not enter any information subject to regulatory protection. This includes:
- Health or medical information
- Financial records or account information
- Legal privilege or legally confidential material
- Information protected under data protection law

---

## Human review requirement

This prototype uses keyword matching, not AI reasoning. Evidence extracted by this tool is
a starting point for human review — not a complete or authoritative policy analysis.

All evidence, briefs, and summaries generated by this tool must be reviewed by a responsible,
qualified human professional before being acted upon.

---

## What this tool is not

This prototype is not:
- Legal advice
- Safeguarding advice or guidance
- HR or employment advice
- Compliance or regulatory advice
- Medical or health advice
- Financial advice
- Academic-integrity advice

Outputs are indicative and for planning and discussion purposes only. Organisations should
seek qualified professional advice for all legal, safeguarding, compliance, HR, and
governance decisions.
