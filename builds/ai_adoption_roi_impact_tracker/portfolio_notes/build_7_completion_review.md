# Build 7 — Completion Review

**AI Adoption ROI and Impact Tracker — Final Phase Review**

---

## 1. Purpose of Build 7

Build 7 demonstrates how a consultant supporting small and medium organisations through AI adoption could:

- Collect and validate structured adoption metrics at workflow level.
- Generate directional ROI estimates using synthetic hourly-rate assumptions.
- Analyse workflow impact and identify training, quality, risk, and efficiency bottlenecks.
- Review staff training completion, confidence change, and adoption readiness.
- Check responsible adoption signals including quality issues, risk incidents, and near misses.
- Convert adoption evidence into structured consulting decisions.
- Produce a client-facing Markdown follow-up report.
- Export all evidence in Markdown, CSV, JSON, PDF, and PNG formats.

All of this is done without real client data, external LLM APIs, databases, authentication, or cloud deployment.

## 3. Consulting Value

Build 7 closes the loop after earlier consulting outputs have been produced. It is designed to answer the question a client is most likely to ask after an AI pilot:

*"Is this working? What should we do next?"*

The eight phases provide a structured, evidence-based answer to that question by tracking whether adoption is producing time savings and confidence gains, identifying where bottlenecks are preventing value, checking whether risk signals are within safe limits, reaching explicit consulting decisions, and producing a client-ready follow-up document.

The Export Centre makes this actionable: the consultant does not need to format or translate the evidence. They can download a Markdown report, a CSV evidence pack, and a JSON file in a single step and bring them directly into a client review meeting.

## 4. How It Connects to Earlier Builds

**Build 1 — AI Readiness and Workflow Audit Tool**

Build 7 is the natural follow-up to Build 1. Build 1 identifies candidate workflows and readiness gaps before adoption begins. Build 7 tracks what happened after those workflows were piloted, and shows whether the readiness gaps Build 1 identified have materialised as quality issues, near misses, or governance concerns.

**Build 4 — AI Staff Training and Workshop Generator**

Build 7 Phase 4 tracks training completion rates and confidence scores directly. If Build 4 training materials are being used, the Phase 4 readiness review provides structured evidence of whether they are producing measurable improvements in staff readiness and confidence.

**Build 5 — AI Consulting Report Generator**

Build 5 produces a report at the beginning of an engagement. Build 7 Phase 7 produces a follow-up report after the pilot has run. Together they form a consulting narrative from first audit to post-pilot review. The Phase 8 Export Centre packages this into downloadable formats that could be attached to a Build 5 client report.

**Build 6 — AI Governance Policy Checker**

Build 7 Phase 5 tracks risk incidents, near misses, and governance review requirements. If Build 6 has reviewed the client's governance policies, Phase 5 of Build 7 provides post-pilot evidence of whether those policies are holding up in practice, or whether new controls are required.

---

## 5. Limitations

- All records are synthetic fictional examples. No real adoption data is tracked.
- ROI figures are based on synthetic hourly-rate assumptions and should not be presented to clients as audited financial savings.
- Decision outcomes are deterministic and template-based. They supplement, not replace, direct consulting judgement.
- PDF and PNG exports are functional but basic. They are not branded and do not use advanced layout or styling.
- There is no input mechanism for real data. A production version of this tool would need validated CSV or form-based data entry.
- There is no trend analysis across multiple adoption periods. Phase 8 closes Build 7 without time-series capability.

---

## 6. Recommended Future Extensions

- Validated CSV or form-based data entry for real adoption records.
- Trend analysis comparing adoption metrics across two or more review periods.
- Branded PDF report templates for specific client sectors (healthcare, education, advice services).
- Integration with Build 1 readiness audit to provide end-to-end traceability from initial assessment to post-pilot review.
- A governance checklist linking Build 6 policy outputs to Build 7 adoption decisions.
- Multi-period confidence tracking to show whether staff confidence is improving over time.
- Automated alert flags for workflows where risk signals are increasing between review periods.
