## Introduction

This checklist must be completed before any AI-generated content is used, shared, submitted, or acted upon. It applies to all content produced with the assistance of AI tools, including ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, or any equivalent product.

Human review is not optional. BrightPath Skills Training requires all staff to take professional responsibility for the content they produce and use, regardless of whether AI tools were involved in its production.

---

## Section A: Data and Privacy Checks

Before submitting any content to an AI tool, confirm:

- [ ] No learner names, reference numbers, or identifiable information were entered into the AI tool.
- [ ] No safeguarding, welfare, or sensitive case information was entered into the AI tool.
- [ ] No staff personal data or HR information was entered into the AI tool.
- [ ] No confidential client or partner information was entered into the AI tool.
- [ ] Any scenario examples used in prompts were fictional or fully anonymised.

If any of the above cannot be confirmed, do not proceed. Report the concern to the Data Protection Lead.

---

## Section B: Accuracy and Factual Checks

After receiving AI output, confirm:

- [ ] All factual claims in the output have been checked against reliable sources.
- [ ] Any references to regulations, policies, statistics, or named sources have been independently verified.
- [ ] Any dates or figures mentioned are current and accurate for this organisation.
- [ ] No information that is specific to BrightPath has been invented or assumed by the AI tool.
- [ ] The content does not contradict known BrightPath policies, procedures, or quality standards.

If any factual claim cannot be verified, it must be removed or replaced with verified information before the content is used.

---

## Section C: Appropriateness and Tone

Confirm:

- [ ] The language is appropriate for the intended audience (learners, staff, commissioners, external partners).
- [ ] The tone is consistent with BrightPath's professional standards and values.
- [ ] The content does not contain stereotyping, discriminatory language, or potentially offensive material.
- [ ] The reading level is appropriate for the target audience.
- [ ] The content does not make promises, guarantees, or claims that BrightPath cannot substantiate.
- [ ] Safeguarding-relevant language is accurate, careful, and does not minimise risk.

---

## Section D: Completeness and Fitness for Purpose

Confirm:

- [ ] The content covers all of the required points for its purpose.
- [ ] There are no obvious gaps that could mislead or leave a reader without important information.
- [ ] The structure and format are appropriate for the intended use.
- [ ] Any required sections, headings, or fields have been included.
- [ ] The content could stand alone as a professional BrightPath document without revealing that it was AI-drafted.

---

## Section E: Decision-Making and Accountability

Confirm:

- [ ] The AI-generated content is not being used to make or inform any of the following decisions without additional expert review: assessment grades, safeguarding referrals, disciplinary actions, complaints outcomes, funding eligibility, or learner welfare decisions.
- [ ] The staff member producing or approving the content has the appropriate professional competence to review and take responsibility for it.
- [ ] The content has been reviewed by the appropriate approver as required by BrightPath's quality processes (curriculum lead, line manager, DSL, or Principal as applicable).

---

## Section F: Record Keeping

Confirm:

- [ ] The use of AI assistance has been noted in the document version history or footer where required.
- [ ] The name of the reviewing staff member and the date of review have been recorded.
- [ ] The approved final version has been saved in the correct location on the shared drive.
- [ ] Any unapproved draft versions have been deleted or archived in the appropriate drafts folder.

---

## Sign-Off

By completing this checklist and approving the content, the staff member confirms that:

- They have reviewed the content against all applicable sections of this checklist.
- They take professional responsibility for the accuracy, appropriateness, and fitness for purpose of the content.
- They have not relied solely on AI output without independent review.
- They are satisfied that no personal data was disclosed to an AI tool in the production of this content.
